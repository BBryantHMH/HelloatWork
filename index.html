From the Hello Mental Health team

Hello — and welcome.

We lead with our hearts, and value humans above everything else.

We built this library for the 900+ humans doing the work at Talbert House. You support people in the hardest moments of their lives, across five service lines and dozens of locations. This site is our way of making sure the resources you need — for yourself, for your team, and for your clients — are in one trusted place. Nothing here is generic. Everything is meant to meet you where you are.

Talbert House at a glance

Five service lines. One community of care.

From addictions to housing, from court & corrections to mental health and primary care, Talbert House has helped Southwest Ohioans since 1965. This library is designed to support every one of those service lines.

🏠

Housing

Safe, stable housing including recovery and reentry housing programs.

💧

Addictions

Outpatient, residential, and jail-based SUD treatment with MAT.

❤

Mental health

Counseling, crisis services, and integrated behavioral healthcare.

⚖

Court & corrections

Reintegration services for justice-involved adults.

👥

Community care

Case management, fatherhood, and community well-being programs.

💉

Primary care

Integrated primary medical care for whole-person wellness.

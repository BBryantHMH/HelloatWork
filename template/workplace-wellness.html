Program pillars

Four ways we support your team.

Every element of this program was designed with behavioral health workers in mind — not repurposed from a generic EAP.

🔒

Confidential 1:1 support

Access to licensed Hello Mental Health psychologists for private consultation — whether you need a referral, a sounding board, or just someone outside your org to talk to.

Completely confidential · No manager notification · No case notes shared
📋

Workshops & trainings

Live and recorded sessions on burnout, secondary trauma, trauma-informed supervision, boundaries, and more — tailored to your organization’s service areas and staff roles.

Scheduled quarterly · Custom topics available · CEU eligible
🗣

Facilitated peer groups

Small, facilitated group sessions for staff to process the weight of the work together — structured by role or service line. Not a therapy group. A human space.

Facilitated by licensed psychologists · Role-specific cohorts · Monthly cadence
🔍

Reflective supervision support

Resources and coaching for supervisors who want to hold space for the clinical experience of their staff — not just the caseload. Includes a supervision toolkit and consultation access.

For supervisors & team leads · Toolkit included · Consultation available

How to access the program

Everything is confidential. Your manager is never notified of individual participation. All services are included in your organization’s Hello at Work agreement.

Get in touch
  1. Browse the tools library below to find something useful right now.
  2. Email welcome@hellomentalhealth.com to schedule a confidential consultation, join a group, or ask about workshops.
  3. Talk to your program coordinator if your org has a designated point of contact for scheduling group sessions.
  4. Check the podcast and video library for content you can access on your own time.
👔 Manager & supervisor toolkit

Are you a supervisor or team lead? There’s a dedicated section in the tools library below. Use the Managers & supervisors filter chip to find resources built specifically for you — including a reflective supervision guide, a team check-in protocol, and a burnout early-warning checklist.

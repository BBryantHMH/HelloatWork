How it works

From signed agreement to live site in weeks, not months.

Hello Mental Health handles setup, content, and ongoing updates. Your staff gets a polished, always-current resource hub.

1

Discovery call

We learn about your organization, staff structure, service areas, and what wellness gaps your team is feeling most.

2

Brand & content setup

We customize the site with your logo and colors, build the provider list, and populate the initial content library.

3

Staff launch

We send an all-staff announcement email, provide a coordinator guide, and host a live Q&A for your team.

4

Ongoing partnership

Monthly new content drops, quarterly program reviews, and a dedicated Hello Mental Health point of contact year-round.

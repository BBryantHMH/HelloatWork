Workplace wellness program

Care for the people who carry the work.

Talbert House and Hello Mental Health built this program for the staff who hold space for clients in the hardest moments of their lives. Confidential support, reflective spaces, and practical tools — designed for the realities of behavioral-health work, not generic wellness advice.

Explore the program Jump to tools
What's included

Four pillars, one program.

Every Talbert House staff member has access to all four pillars at no cost. Use what fits the moment — you don’t have to engage with everything to get something from it.

💬

Confidential 1:1 sessions

Up to eight free sessions per year with an HMH-vetted therapist outside the Talbert House system. Use them for therapy, coaching, or a single hard week. Schedule yourself; no supervisor involvement; HR never sees who used what.

Self-scheduled · Fully confidential · In-person or telehealth
👣

Reflective supervision groups

Monthly small-group spaces facilitated by an HMH clinician, organized by service line. Process the work with peers who actually get it — the kind of supervision that prevents burnout instead of just naming it.

Monthly · 90 minutes · Service-line cohorts of 6–8
🎯

Workshops & live sessions

Quarterly themed sessions on burnout, trauma stewardship, boundary-setting, and reflective supervision. Attend live, or watch the recordings later in the Videos library. Each session includes a take-home practice you can actually use.

Quarterly · Recorded · Live attendance counts as CEU-eligible
📚

The resource library

This site. On-demand tools, vetted external resources, the curated provider directory, plus writing, video, and podcast content from the Hello Mental Health team. Designed to be useful in five minutes or fifty.

Always available · Updated monthly · Internal use only

How to engage

You don’t need a referral or a reason. Pick the door that feels right today.

  1. Want a 1:1 session? Email Welcome@HelloMentalHealth.com with your preferred days/times. We’ll match you to a therapist within 5 business days.
  2. Curious about supervision groups? Talk to your service-line lead, or email us — we can let you know when the next cohort opens.
  3. Want a quick tool right now? Skip to the tools library below.
